VAST Digitisation Tool
The VAST Digitisation Tool

The VAST Digitisation Tool is an application that supports the digitisation of educational activities.
The tool supports:

  1. the definition of activities of multiple events and steps, as well as of the stimuli related to each step,
  2. the digitisation of activities' products including both raw data (such as digitised images) and the declaration of statements.
It also allows to include for each activity general information, such as target group characteristics, the companion handbook and related sources to Europeana.
